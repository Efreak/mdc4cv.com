Refurbished Equipment

 

Materials Development Corporation has manufactured C-V plotting equipment for over 30 years.  With all of the MDC systems in the field, we occasionally get systems back in trade for newer systems.  MDC engineers evaluate the returned equipment to see if it is still of value to our customers. 

We make these systems available to our customers on a first come-first served basis.  

  • All meters are checked, repaired as needed, and calibrated.  

  • Computers are checked, cleaned, and upgraded as needed.  

  • Hot chuck systems are evaluated, cleaned, and repaired as needed.

  • Software is upgraded and tested.

All refurbished equipment comes with a 90 day  warranty on parts and labor.  Longer warranties are available.

If you need a good working (if slightly used) C-V Plotter or other MDC measurement system or accessory, contact an engineer at MDC and see what we have to offer.  If we don't have what you need today, we may have it tomorrow.
