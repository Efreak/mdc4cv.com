Measurement Services

MDC offers measurement services based on the equipment and software that we manufacture.  Whether to supplement in-house measurements or to verify equipment operation, MDC can provide these measurements:

  • C-V plotting for semiconductor devices

  • Dit tests for MOS devices

  • CVBT tests for mobile ion measurements

  • TVS tests for high sensitivity mobile ion measurements and ionic species detection

  • Dielectric Constant tests for insulators

  • Current-Voltage and GOI measurements for insulators

  • Doping Profile tests for implants, epi, semi-insulating, HEMT, and bulk material

  • Photovoltaic parameters, effeciency

  • Electrochemical measurements such as Cyclic Voltammetry and Galvanostatic Charging

  • SILC (Stress-Induced Leakage Current)

  • DLCP (Drive Level Capacitance Profiling)

  • Other specialty measurements.

Refer to our SOFTWARE section for more services.

 Difficult to measure devices are our specialty!
