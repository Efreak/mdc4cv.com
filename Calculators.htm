MDC CALCULATORS

The following calculators make some common computations that can simplify your analysis, just as MDC measurement systems can simplify your measurements.

Permittivity Calculator

The MDC Permittivity Calculator uses the parallel plate capacitance formula to compute the relationship between dielectric constant (permittivity), capacitance, contact area, and oxide thickness.  Simply input the values for three of the four variables to calculate the unknown.  Be sure to zero out the required field.

 

Breakdown Voltage

The MDC Breakdown Calculator will calculate the theoretical bulk breakdown voltage of silicon material based on the doping level.  Simply input either of the variables to calculate the other value.  Be sure to zero out the required field.  Valid for silicon only.

 

Depletion - Depth

The MDC Depletion - Depth Calculator will calculate the depletion width, doping, or voltage level.  Input any two of the three variables to calculate the unknown value.  Be sure to zero out the required field.  Valid for silicon only.

 

Doping - Resistivity

The MDC Doping - Resistivity Converter uses ASTM formulae to convert between doping and resistivity for silicon.  Simply input the value of either doping or resistivity, along with the material type (N or P), and the calculator will output the unknown value.  Be sure to zero out the required field.  Valid for silicon only.
